About Me



Shreya Budhathoki is a 19-year old college student who also involves herself in share marketing and photography. She has been interested in photography since 2014 and involves herself with photographs of beautiful natural scenaries as a hobby. Although still a teenager, she's been actively involved in share marketing for a year ot two now. Being a student of computer science, she has had courses on programming languages such as HTML, QBasic and JAVA.


Shreya has passed her schooling year from a well-reputed school in her country - Graded English Medium School(GEMS). After that she attended St.Xavier's College for her high school studies but then shortly transferred to Nobel Higher Secondary School from where she graduated her high school. Currently, for her undergraduate course, she has joined NAMI college which is affiliated with the University of Northampton in England, for a honours degree course in Computer Science.


As a little kid back in the school days, Shreya was awarded with a certificate of superstudent award as a recognition for her achievements in academics. She has also received various certificates for her outstanding performance in ECA activities as a gymnast and has also been awarded for doing a philanthropic deed in helping raise "Garnet Fund" which is used to sponser an underpriviledged child.
